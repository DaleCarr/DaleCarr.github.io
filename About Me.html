About Me

Who I am:

I am Dale, I'm 22. I've played games as long as I can remember, both table-top and computer games. This interest in games is what led me into an IT career when I discovered that games don't just appear, they're created, and more importantly, you can learn how. My passion for games is what drives me in this career, and helps me to find the fun in every programming task.

What I've done:

Left college with a BTEC level 3 in IT and a B in IFS financial studies. Later graduated from the University of Essex with a 2:1 in computer games. Built a few java projects for fun, mainly gaming tools.

What I want to do:

Biggest ambition is to lead design on a game. Being in control of the thematic elements to a game, and leading a team to success is one of my main career goals. Aside from that, I enjoy seeing the IT sector from as many sides as possile, so a senior developer role would also suit me.
